Gonca Yilmaz

Hi, I am an ML Engineer at Google Zurich working on Core Web Ranking. I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Bilkent University and a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from University of Zurich. I wrote my Master's Thesis at Computer Vision Group from ETH Zurich. During my studies, I had the opportunity to intern 3 times at Google and 2 times at Amazon with various ML teams. My last internship as an Applied Scientist intern at Amazon was on warehouse automation using state-of-the-art computer vision methods with Amazon Fulfillment Technologies. I also had the chance to work as a teaching assistant at ETH Zurich for 3 semesters.
